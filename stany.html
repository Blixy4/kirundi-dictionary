The idea of writing an English–Kirundi dictionary came to me when I noticed that many people in my community had problems translating between English and Kirundi.

🌍 International Projects

Stany contributed to the English–Kirundi Dictionary as English Facilitator and co-author of translated entries. He also worked with the Vodafone Foundation – IRC English learning program (2019–2020), facilitating cross-community English education across East Africa, and with Good Neighbors in a monitoring and evaluation role for community development programs.

💬 A Word to Our Users

My goal in writing this dictionary is to provide a reliable reference source for learners, teachers, and researchers, and to support my community in overcoming language barriers. I believe this resource will help students, teachers, and anyone interested in learning either language. It is a privilege to contribute something lasting to this cause.
